Technology & Features FAQ

What is a pooper scooper service?

A pooper scooper service is a professional dog waste removal company that comes to your home or property to clean up dog poop on a recurring schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, or one-time). At Scoop Unit, we keep your yard clean, safe, and family-friendly so you don't have to handle the mess.

How much does Scoop Unit cost?

Pricing depends on the number of dogs, yard size, and service frequency. We offer affordable weekly, bi-weekly, and one-time cleanup options. Get a personalized quote instantly with our Free Estimate Tool.

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve Crestview, Destin, Pensacola, Niceville, Fort Walton Beach, Navarre, Gulf Breeze, and surrounding Florida Panhandle communities.

Do I need to be home when Scoop Unit comes?

Nope! As long as we have access to your yard, you don't need to be home. Our field techs will handle the cleanup and update your account once the job is complete.
