How much does dog waste removal cost?

Pricing depends on the number of dogs, yard size, and service frequency. Most Florida homes start around $15-30 per visit. Get your exact estimate instantly.

Do you service all Florida Panhandle areas?

We serve Crestview, Destin, Pensacola, Niceville, Fort Walton Beach, Navarre, Gulf Breeze and surrounding communities. View complete coverage map.

Is weekly or bi-weekly service better?

Weekly service is most popular for families with kids and multiple pets. Bi-weekly works well for smaller yards. Compare options.

Do I need to be home during service?

No! As long as we have yard access, you don't need to be home. We'll update your account when service is complete.
